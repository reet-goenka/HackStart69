Our Mission

Resource Sharing Hub is a student-driven platform built to simplify the exchange of academic resources. Our goal is to help students collaborate and grow by sharing study notes, PDFs, useful links, and subject-specific materials.


What You Can Do

  • Upload notes, PDFs, and links easily.
  • Browse study materials by subject.
  • Search and discover resources uploaded by others.

Who We Are

This platform was developed as part of a hackathon to encourage knowledge sharing and collaboration in the student community. It's designed by students, for students.
